My Musical Instruments

My collection consists of four musical instruments. Each instrument is unique and most are antique.

Yairi DY-138 Acoustic Guitar

This accoustic is low cost for its high quality materials which include mahogany, rosewood, and a bone bridge.

Wurlitzer Theater Organ

This antique organ has a wide array of sounds. It is equipped with an orbit III synthesizer. This early synthesizer is limited in today's world but still has plenty of options to make unique and novel voices.

Hohner Echo Harmonica

This rare harmonica is over sixty years old. It simultaneously plays two octaves and has a great vintage sound.

Unknown Classical Accoustic Guitar

This guitar is equipped with nylon strings and is built in the classical style. While purchased for under $100, the quality and sound are of a mid-priced accoustic guitar. It bears no manufacturers marks, so its make is unknown. It is the only one of my instruments that was also a gift.
